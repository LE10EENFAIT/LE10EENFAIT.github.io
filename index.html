Research

I am working on convergent approximation methods for global optimization. My main research interests are measure theory, functional analysis, flows, and learning theory. I am currently working on my thesis with Nicolas Vayatis. I am also learning about the proof assistant Lean and I'm trying to promote it in my laboratory.
