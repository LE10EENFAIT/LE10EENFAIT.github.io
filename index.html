Research

I am working on convergent stochastic approximation methods for global optimization. My research interests are measure theory, probability theory, flows, optimal transport, and stochastic algorithms in general. I am currently working on my thesis with Nicolas Vayatis. I am also learning about the proof assistant Lean and I'm trying to promote it in my laboratory.
